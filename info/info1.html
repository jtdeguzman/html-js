The area around the wrecks has rock formations which provide for snorkeling opportunities, with underwater visibility extending up to 80 feet. The water is often calm. Coron is one of the most visited destinations for wreck diving in the Philippines. Wreck dive sites are found in depths as shallow as 10 to 30 feet and as deep as 120 to 140 feet. Most are in the range of about 60 to 80 feet.
